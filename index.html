HOW TO PLAY:


Number of players: 2 - 3


After clicking "It's Go Time" a word will be presented to you which player 1 will need to draw using the canvas tools (brush size, colour, fill). Once satisfied, or the timer runs out, the picture will be saved and the game will move to the next picture to draw.


It is now either player 1 (again) or player 2 (if 3 players) to draw the second image just like the first one. Once done it will be time for the final player to make their guess


The two wonderfully drawn images will be displayed (which can be expanded) to the guessing player. This player must use the two images to guess the combined (compound) word that the images make up.


Whether the guess was correct or not will be displayed after making the guess. At this point it's time to play again!


HAVE FUN!
